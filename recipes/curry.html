Chicken Curry

Home

Description

Chicken curry is a rich and flavorful dish made with spices, onions, garlic, ginger, and tender pieces of chicken.

It is commonly served with rice or roti and is a staple in many South Asian households.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt and oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and spices.
  3. Add chicken and cook until sealed.
  4. Add tomatoes and simmer.
  5. Cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Serve hot with rice.
