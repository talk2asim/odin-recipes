Chow Mein

Home

Description

Chow Mein is a popular stir-fried noodle dish made with vegetables, soy sauce, and optional chicken or beef.

It is quick to prepare and packed with flavor, making it a favorite street food and homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • Carrots
  • Cabbage
  • Bell peppers
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and sauté garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until slightly tender.
  4. Add noodles and soy sauce.
  5. Stir-fry everything together for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.
