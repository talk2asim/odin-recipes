Descriptions

Momo is a popular Nepali dumpling filled with seasoned meat or vegetables. It is usually steamed and served with spicy tomato achar.

Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, momo is a comfort food loved across Nepal and parts of India and Tibet.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Ground chicken or buff
  • Onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare dough using flour and water.
  2. Mix ground meat with chopped vegetables and spices.
  3. Roll small dough circles.
  4. Add filling and fold into dumpling shape.
  5. Steam for 10–12 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with spicy tomato achar.
